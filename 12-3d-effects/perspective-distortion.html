Scroll Distortion
I

Perspective on Scroll

As elements enter and leave the viewport, they rotate and shift in 3D space, creating a dynamic scrolling experience that keeps users engaged longer.

II

Rotation Mapping

Each card's X rotation is mapped to its scroll position relative to the viewport center — above center tilts back, below tilts forward, creating a natural spatial flow.

III

Combined Influence

Mouse position combines with scroll position for compound distortion — move your cursor to one side while scrolling to see dramatic warping effects.

IV

Opacity Fade

Cards fade in as they approach the viewport center and fade as they leave, ensuring focus stays on the most visible content.
