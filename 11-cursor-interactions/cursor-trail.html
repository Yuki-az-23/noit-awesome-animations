Cursor Trail Effects

Move your mouse to see mesmerizing trail effects. Switch between three unique trail styles below.

How It Works

  • Move your cursor around the page to see the active trail style
  • Click buttons to switch between Classic Dots, Gradient Comet, and Magnetic Lines
  • Hover over interactive elements to see the cursor grow
  • All rendering happens on a fixed canvas at 60fps using requestAnimationFrame
