02 — Circular Mode

A ring that wraps your progress

The SVG circle indicator in the bottom-right corner provides constant at-a-glance feedback. Click it to scroll back to the top.

🔄

SVG Dash Array

Uses stroke-dashoffset manipulation for smooth ring animation.

🖱️

Clickable

Click the circle to instantly scroll back to the top of the page.

✨

Glow Effect

Progress stroke adds a soft color glow at higher scroll positions.

📏

Scaled Ring

Circumference is calculated from radius for pixel-perfect accuracy.

03 — Bar Mode

Linear clarity

The top progress bar provides a familiar linear representation. Its gradient fill shifts hue as you approach the end, creating a sense of momentum.

🌈

Gradient Fill

Two-color gradient that transitions along the bar length.

💡

Subtle Glow

🎯

Pinpoint

⚡

Performant
