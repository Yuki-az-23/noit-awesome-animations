Overlay Menu Animation

Click the hamburger icon to open a full-screen overlay menu. Each item staggers in from a unique direction with elastic easing. The background blurs and darkens behind the menu. Close reverses the stagger.

Features

Staggered directional entry • Elastic cubic-bezier easing • Backdrop blur & darken • Reverse-stagger close • Hover underline animation • Decorative side text reveals
